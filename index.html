Fast Food Nutrition Calculator FAQs

  • How accurate is this nutrition data? We use brand datasets where available, but values can change by region, recipe updates, and serving size.
  • Why do calories differ between countries? Portions and ingredients vary by market, so the same item name can have different nutrition values.
  • Does this tool calculate macros? Yes. It totals calories, protein, carbs, fat, sugar, and sodium for your selected items.
  • Can I use this for medical decisions? No. This tool is for general information only and is not medical advice.
  • What does “Linked” mean? It means the selected brand and country dataset loaded successfully.
  • Can I add multiple quantities of the same item? Yes. Use the plus and minus controls in Your Meal.
  • Why is an item missing? Menus differ by region. Try another country dataset or search using fewer words.
  • Do you store my meal data? No. Your selections are calculated in your browser only.
  • Which brands are supported? Starbucks, McDonald’s, and KFC.
  • Is this calculator free? Yes. It is free to use.
